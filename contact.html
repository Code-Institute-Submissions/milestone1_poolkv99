Contact us

If you want some information about biliards in general, you want to start playing or you have played before and are searching for a team to join, please feel free to contact us. If you are new to the game we will gladly invite you to come join a trainingsession with us.


We train every Wednesday evening arround 20:00 (CET) at the POOL'KV in Apeldoorn. This is our team-trainingsession, but most of us are there 4-5 times in the week. Come join us and don't be afraid to have a go at it!


We hope to hear from you or welcome you at the POOL'KV. !
