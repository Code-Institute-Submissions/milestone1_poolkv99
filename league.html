Northern 2th National League

We play in the 2th division Northern National League, which contains 8 teams in total. There is a home and an away match against each opponent.


Each match consists of 7 games played: two 9-ball, two 8-ball, a 10-ball, a straight(14.1-ball) and a couple 10-ball(2v2). For each game won your team gets a point. If at the end of the competition there are teams with the same amount of points, the mutual result will determine the difference. If this is also a draw, then the number of frames won will count.
