Who are we?

We are 6 biliards players who play teamcompetition together in our team Pool'KV 99. The team in its current composition has been together for 5 years. We play in the 2th division North.


The team exists for 6 years now and some of us know eachother for a long time before that. In 2014 Robin and Robbert played for Pool'KV 1, but they were looking for another team. A team from another city were looking for a new location to play and practice. After a few meetings we created Pool'KV 99.


Our main goal is to have fun and be competitive, but not at all costs.
