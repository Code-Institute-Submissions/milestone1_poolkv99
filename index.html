Who are we?

We are 6 billards players who play team competition together in our team Pool'KV 99. The team in its current composition has been together for 5 years. We play in the 2nd division North.
Our main goal is to have fun and be competitive, but not at all costs.

The team exists for 6 years now, and some of us know each other for a long time before that. In 2014 Robin and Robbert played for Pool'KV 1, but they were looking for another team. A team from another city (Jeroen and Mozes) were looking for a new location to play and practice. After a few meetings we created Pool'KV 99. But with four players we still didn't have enough players for starting in the competition. So we reached out to some players who were inactive, and Bas and Jacob soon joined our team. We have since been a team and a force to reckon with.
